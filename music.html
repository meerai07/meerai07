Music is one of my passions.

I have been learning Carnatic vocal music for 12 years and Carnatic Veena for 8 years. I have also been an occasional guest performer at the Eternity Band, a local music club.

Playing and singing Carnatic music unleash my creativity, and relax me while stimulating my brain at the same time.

  • Janani Mamava by Swati Tirunal in Bhairava Raga
  • Varnam in Hamsadhwani Raga (highlights only),
  • Varnam in Shankarabharanam (vocal) (highlights only)
  • Opening act as Emcee at Fremont Library, Thillana by Lalgudi Jayaraman in Maand Raga
